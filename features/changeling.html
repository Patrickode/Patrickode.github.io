Changeling

Role: Mother's Level Lead
Platform: Unreal, Blueprints

Changeling is a narrative mystery game for VR. I and two others were tasked with taking the half-finished Mother's Level to a state of near completion over the summer; I was the primary point of communication between the project director, Elouise Oyzon, and my fellow leads for the other levels.

  • I made the blueprints for the antagonistic force of the mother's level; for example, the anim BP for a shadowy hand with a parameterized duration and position for its inverse kinematics.
  • I delved deep into the Niagara particle system for the first time, making a towering cumulonimbus-shaped mass of black fog using a custom module and trigonometry functions
  • Despite lack of ability to set a beam particle system's source and target to actors, I discovered a way to link both ends of the player's tether beams to moving, dynamic locations, without the beam having to "catch up" to either regardless of their speed
