Changeling

Role: Mother's Level Lead
Platform: Unreal, Blueprints

Changeling is a narrative mystery game for VR. I and two others were tasked with taking the fairly finished Mother's Level to a state of near completion over the summer; I was the primary point of communication between the project director, Elouise Oyzon, and my fellow leads for the other levels.

  • I made the blueprints for the antagonistic force of the mother's level; the anim BP for the shadowy hand with custom duration and position for its inverse kinematics, the BP for communicating with the level blueprint at large about that hand's state, and so on.
  • I delved deep into the Niagara particle system for the first time, making a towering cumulonimbus-shaped mass of black fog using a custom module and trigonometry functions
  • Despite lack of ability to set a beam particle system's source and target to actors, I discovered a way to link both ends of the player's tether beams to moving, dynamic locations, without the beam having to "catch up" to either regardless of their speed
